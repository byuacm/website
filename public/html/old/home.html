What is BYU ACM?

ACM, the Association for Computing Machinery, is a national organization for computer science professionals. BYU ACM is one of hundreds of local student chapters of ACM. The goal of these chapters is to provide networking opportunities to students and allow them to develop skills that will prepare them for a professional career.
